About Us

Yashwanti Group is a National, diversified organisation of 5 major companies namely Yashwanti Transports, Tuljabhavani Cotton Trading company, Yashwanti Electricals, Yashwanti Agro, Yashwanti Infrastructures. Group was established in 1992 by Hon. B. R. GADHE & Hon R. R. GADHE. We deliver end-to-end solutions in 3 business segments, namely Engineering & Construction, Infrastructure and Energy. Developing megastructures and iconic landmarks, our dedicated workforce of over 135 people in over India is focused on sustainable development.
